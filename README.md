## Projects

- Bird Talk [private]
  - A full-stack application for curating livestream interactivity that simplifies the complicated parts and doesn't break when the show is busy.
  - I consulted clients, users, and operators in order to create a fluid and powerful user experience.
  - Has better uptime than [GitHub](https://mrshu.github.io/github-statuses/).
- [ff-lucid](https://github.com/bost-ty/ff-lucid)
  - The Firefox port of Daniel Eden's [Lucid](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/lucid/achogfadpkcepkepcpegehpiiioihmik), a Chrome extension.
  - It's a low-key persistent, synced notepad in your new tab.
  - Supports additional user settings, Firefox Sync, and handles Firefox installs that are configured for security/privacy.
- [Dynomix](dyno.bostwick.dev) 
  - Dynomix is a *dynamic* vision *mix*er assistant in the browser for vMix and Bitfocus Companion.
  - Designed to reduce cognitive overhead for technical directors building their projects, preventing hard-to-troubleshoot errors and making it easier to get to the interesting stuff (like actually building the show).
- Bird Suite [private]
  - A frontend-based Twitch analytics app that enables event tagging, knows the difference between a "viewer" and a "chatter", and makes it simple to find out what makes people join and watch a Twitch livestream.


A lot of my *really fun and cool* work is client-based and not public, but I hope you'll take a look around my projects/repos and see what looks cool to you.
